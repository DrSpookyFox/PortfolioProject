About me

I started climbing in college at a free outdoor climbing wall with no instruction and in bare feetand I would like to think that I have improved since. I became manager after I worked at GP81 for a week, noticed we didn’t have a schedule, and then suggested we have a schedule. My job at the gym is to make sure all operations are running smoothly and continue to improve our space through our staff, facility, and community. I don’t have any climbing accomplishments and probably never will, but I love bouldering outside and will bring the best snacks. I like to spend my free time cooking and eating food, hanging out with my dog Lemon, and listening to podcasts.
